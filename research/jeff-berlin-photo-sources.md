## Search Strategy Notes

- `Jeff Berlin` is noisy because there are unrelated people and sites with the same name.
- Better queries:
  - `"Jeff Berlin" bassist photo`
  - `"Jeff Berlin" bass player live`
  - `"Jeff Berlin" Bx3 photo`
  - `"Jeff Berlin" NAMM`
  - `"Jeff Berlin" Allan Holdsworth`
  - `"Jeff Berlin" Bill Bruford`
  - `"Jeff Berlin" Jack Songs photo`
  - `site:gettyimages.com "Jeff Berlin" bass`
  - `site:commons.wikimedia.org "Jeff Berlin"`

## Constraints

- "All photos" is not realistically provable from a public-web search alone.
- Many photos are likely behind social feeds, agency search walls, or image-CDN pages with weak indexing.
- Rights vary heavily. Public discoverability is not the same as permission to reuse.
